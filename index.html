Key Responsibilities

  • • Read and analyzed RTL design files and technology libraries.
  • • Created and validated SDC constraints including clocks, input/output delays, false paths, multicycle paths, and clock uncertainties.
  • • Performed synthesis using Synopsys Design Compiler.
  • • Generated gate-level netlists and timing reports.
  • • Analyzed setup timing violations and optimized critical paths.
  • • Evaluated QoR reports for timing, area, and power.

Achievements Summary

  • Achieved 100% setup timing closure with zero violations.
  • Defined and validated 20+ timing constraints.
  • Reduced datapath delay by 15%.

  • Reduced area utilization by 8% through synthesis.
